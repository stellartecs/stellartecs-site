PRODUCTS


NesherAI

NesherAI is an intelligence gathering and mass surveillance tool, designed to support law enforcement and community security organizations in tracking terrorists, threat actors, and violent criminals. It is also marketed towards NGOs, activist groups, companies, and universities for profiling individuals with malicious intent, terrorist affiliations and online criminal behavior. It combines a searchable database and a facial recognition tool to track and counteract growing threats.

CyberBird

Currently in the research and development stage, the CyberBird is designed to be a revolutionary surveillance and all-purpose signals intelligence drone used for electronic and cyber warfare and military operations in the fields of air traffic signals, cellular networking, radio communications, Wi-Fi networks, Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), and Sub-GHz transmissions.


Shomer

With the upcoming future threats to modern cryptography and internet security and the rise of post-quantum cryptographic schemes, Shomer is a next generation Hardware Security Module (HSM) designed to provide post-quantum cryptographic security capabilities, both symmetric and asymmetric, and to adapt to the upcoming future shifts in cryptographic technology demands.
